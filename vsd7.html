Want to be a better speaker?

Here's a tip that might sound counterintuitive:

Stop speaking. Start observing.
  • Watch how people listen.
  • Watch how their eyebrows rise at a certain word.
  • Watch when they nod.
  • Watch when they drift away.

Every room tells a story. Great speakers learn to read it.

Think of the best speakers you've seen.

They don't just speak well ‚Äî they react well.

They pick up on what the audience is thinking before anyone says a word.

That's not talent. That's training. You can train that too.
