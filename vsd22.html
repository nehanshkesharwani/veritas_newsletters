When you think of Nike, you probably think of its swoosh, athletes, or the slogan "Just Do It."

But behind this global brand is the story of Phil Knight, a shy runner from Oregon who turned a crazy idea into one of the world's most iconic movements. His journey holds a powerful lesson for anyone who wants to speak, lead, or inspire.

Phil didn't just sell shoes out of his car‚Äîhe sold the idea of better performance and pushing limits.

3 Overcome "No" With Storytelling

Phil Knight faced rejection, legal battles, and competition from Adidas and Puma. Yet he kept telling stories about athletes and victory.

Lesson: When you speak about overcoming odds, you inspire others to believe they can, too.
